## How to Use This List

### Step 1: Prioritize by Relevance
Focus first on Nigerian and African platforms, then diaspora-focused sites, then international real estate blogs.

### Step 2: Create Quality Content
Before pitching, ensure you have valuable content to offer — guides, market insights, or expert commentary.

### Step 3: Personalize Your Outreach
Don't mass email. Research each platform and tailor your pitch to their audience and content style.

### Step 4: Track Your Progress
Use a spreadsheet to track:
- Platform name
- Contact date
- Response received
- Link secured
- Follow-up needed

### Step 5: Build Relationships
Link building is relationship building. Engage with content, share posts, and provide value beyond just asking for links.
