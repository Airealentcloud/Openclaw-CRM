## 3) Page Structure Patterns (observed)
**Nigeria Property Centre blog posts**
- H1 title, author/date, breadcrumb microdata
- Intro paragraph + long-form body; many are educational/guide style
- Some posts include **Table of Contents** and numbered sections
- CTAs to browse listings or contact agents

**Villa Afrika blog posts (WordPress)**
- Standard WP article layout: H1, featured image, author/date, category
- Rich headings (H2/H3), internal links to listings and area guides
- JSON-LD schema (WebSite, WebPage, BreadcrumbList, Organization)

**Mshel Homes blog (mshelhomes.com + app.mshelhomes.com)**
- Short/medium posts; CTA to contact/sales
- Minimal visible structured data (no JSON‑LD detected on app.mshelhomes.com article)

**Abuja Properties**
- Listing pages with price, location, specs, and “more listings” blocks
- Thin text on listing pages (1–2 short paragraphs), heavy emphasis on photos
