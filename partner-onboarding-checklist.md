# Partner Onboarding Checklist
## A.I Realent Referral Program

---

## Pre-Registration

### ☐ Step 1: Initial Contact
- [ ] Prospective partner expresses interest
- [ ] Explain program basics (2-3 minutes)
- [ ] Send program overview PDF
- [ ] Schedule follow-up call

### ☐ Step 2: Qualification
- [ ] Verify identity (valid ID)
- [ ] Check for conflicts of interest
- [ ] Confirm bank account details
- [ ] Determine appropriate tier

---

## Registration Process

### ☐ Step 3: Documentation
- [ ] Send referral agreement
- [ ] Partner reviews and signs
- [ ] Collect signed agreement
- [ ] Issue Partner ID (AIP-XXXXX)

### ☐ Step 4: System Setup
- [ ] Create partner profile in CRM
- [ ] Generate unique referral link
- [ ] Add to partner database
- [ ] Set up tracking

### ☐ Step 5: Welcome Package
- [ ] Send welcome email
- [ ] Provide partner ID card (digital/physical)
- [ ] Share partner portal login
- [ ] Add to partner WhatsApp group

---

## First Week

### ☐ Step 6: Orientation
- [ ] Schedule onboarding call (30 min)
- [ ] Walk through partner portal
- [ ] Explain referral process
- [ ] Review commission structure
- [ ] Answer questions

### ☐ Step 7: Training
- [ ] Share training materials
- [ ] Provide sales scripts
- [ ] Explain property portfolio
- [ ] Review target client profiles
- [ ] Share success stories

### ☐ Step 8: Marketing Setup
- [ ] Provide email signature template
- [ ] Share social media graphics
- [ ] Send business card template (if applicable)
- [ ] Give presentation slides
- [ ] Provide FAQ document

---

## First Month

### ☐ Step 9: First Referral
- [ ] Check in weekly
- [ ] Offer support for first referral
- [ ] Process referral promptly
- [ ] Provide feedback

### ☐ Step 10: Engagement
- [ ] Add to monthly newsletter
- [ ] Invite to partner webinar
- [ ] Share market updates
- [ ] Request feedback

### ☐ Step 11: Performance Review
- [ ] Review referral activity
- [ ] Address any challenges
- [ ] Celebrate early wins
- [ ] Adjust approach if needed

---

## Ongoing

### ☐ Monthly
- [ ] Send partner newsletter
- [ ] Share new property alerts
- [ ] Provide market insights
- [ ] Highlight top performers

### ☐ Quarterly
- [ ] Partner satisfaction survey
- [ ] Performance review meeting
- [ ] Training session
- [ ] Networking event

### ☐ Annually
- [ ] Program review
- [ ] Commission rate evaluation
- [ ] Partner appreciation event
- [ ] Agreement renewal

## Partner Success Metrics

### Track These KPIs:
- [ ] Time to first referral
- [ ] Number of referrals per month
- [ ] Conversion rate
- [ ] Revenue generated
- [ ] Partner satisfaction score

## Support Resources

### Partner Has Access To:
- [ ] Dedicated WhatsApp support line
- [ ] Partner email: partners@airealent.ng
- [ ] Partner portal login
- [ ] Training video library
- [ ] Marketing material library
- [ ] Private partner community

---

## Red Flags to Watch

### Partner May Need Support If:
- [ ] No referrals after 30 days
- [ ] Low-quality referrals
- [ ] Communication drops
- [ ] Questions about commission
- [ ] Negative feedback

### Action Steps:
1. Schedule check-in call
2. Identify challenges
3. Provide additional training
4. Offer 1-on-1 support
5. Consider tier adjustment
