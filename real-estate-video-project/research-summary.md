## Pain Points Identified

### Document Verification Issues:
1. **Fraudulent Documents:** Fake C of O, survey plans, governor's consent
2. **Multiple Allocations:** Same land sold to multiple buyers
3. **Delayed Processing:** Lengthy verification timelines (weeks to months)
4. **Inconsistent Standards:** Varying requirements across states/agencies
5. **Lack of Central Database:** Fragmented records across multiple agencies

### Market Distrust Issues:
1. **Diaspora Vulnerability:** Overseas investors particularly targeted
2. **Reputation Damage:** Legitimate developers suffer from market-wide distrust
3. **Price Premiums:** Verified properties command 20-30% premiums
4. **Transaction Delays:** Extended due diligence periods

### Technology Gaps:
1. **Manual Processes:** Paper-based verification systems
2. **Integration Challenges:** Siloed agency databases
3. **Accessibility Issues:** Limited digital access for rural transactions
4. **Authentication Methods:** Reliance on physical stamps and signatures
