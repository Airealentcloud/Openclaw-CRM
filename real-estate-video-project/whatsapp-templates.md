## WhatsApp Call Script (If they agree to call)

**Opening:**
"Hi [Contact Name], thanks for making time. As I mentioned, we're producing a 3-part video series to address Nigeria's land scam problem through education and technology demonstrations."

**Key points to cover:**
1. "The series will feature estates that demonstrate proper verification processes"
2. "We cover all production costs - this is a partnership, not a service sale"
3. "We'll position featured estates as 'Nigeria's Most Verified Properties'"
4. "The series targets diaspora investors and high-net-worth individuals"
5. "We need just 2-3 hours for filming and a brief interview"

**Questions to ask:**
1. "Does this align with your marketing goals?"
2. "What verification processes do you currently have in place?"
3. "Would next month work for filming?"

**Closing:**
"Great, I'll send over a brief partnership outline via email. Looking forward to working together!"

## Response Handling Templates

**If they ask "How much does this cost?":**
"There's no cost to you. We cover all production expenses. This is a partnership where we both benefit from the content creation and distribution."

**If they ask "What's the catch?":**
"No catch! We're building a portfolio of quality estates to showcase in our series. You get professional video content and exposure, we get to feature quality developments that demonstrate verification best practices."

**If they ask "Who owns the content?":**
"Joint ownership. You can use the video for your marketing indefinitely, and we can use it as a case study in our series and promotional materials."

**If they ask "How much time commitment?":**
"Approximately 4-5 hours total: 30-minute intro call, 2-3 hour estate tour for filming, 30-minute interview, and some follow-up coordination."

**If they're interested:**
"Excellent! I'll send you a calendar invite for our next step. What email should I use?"

## Best Practices for WhatsApp Outreach

1. **Timing:** Send messages during business hours (10am-4pm)
2. **Personalization:** Always use their name and reference their company
3. **Brevity:** Keep initial messages under 3 sentences
4. **Value-first:** Lead with what you're offering, not what you need
5. **Follow-up:** Wait 3-5 days before following up
6. **Professional tone:** Even on WhatsApp, maintain professional language
7. **Call to action:** Always end with a clear next step (call, meeting, etc.)
