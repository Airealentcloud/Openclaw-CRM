## TERMS & CONDITIONS

### 1. PROGRAM OVERVIEW

A.I Realent Global Resources Ltd ("Company") operates a referral program ("Program") that rewards individuals and businesses ("Partner") for referring potential property buyers ("Referrals").

### 2. PARTNER ELIGIBILITY

By signing this Agreement, Partner confirms:
- They are 18 years or older
- They have provided accurate personal information
- They have a valid Nigerian bank account
- They are not currently employed by a competing real estate firm
- They will comply with all applicable laws and regulations

### 3. REFERRAL PROCESS

**3.1 Registration**
- Partner receives a unique Partner ID upon registration
- Partner may also receive unique referral links for digital sharing

**3.2 Valid Referrals**
A referral is valid when:
- The referred party is not already in Company database
- The referral is registered before first contact with Company
- The referral mentions Partner ID or uses Partner's referral link

**3.3 Invalid Referrals**
Referrals are invalid if:
- Self-referral (Partner refers themselves)
- Referral is already an existing client
- Referral was previously contacted by Company
- Fraudulent or misleading information provided

### 4. COMMISSION STRUCTURE

**4.1 Commission Rates**

| Property Value | Commission Rate |
|----------------|-----------------|
| ₦10M - ₦50M | 2% |
| ₦50M - ₦100M | 2.5% |
| ₦100M - ₦200M | 3% |
| ₦200M+ | 3.5% |

**4.2 Commission Calculation**
Commission = Final Sale Price × Commission Rate

**4.3 Payment Terms**
- Commission paid within 14 days of deal closing
- Deal closing = Full payment received from buyer
- Payment via bank transfer only
- 5% withholding tax deducted as per Nigerian law

**4.4 Payment Schedule**
- Partner notified within 48 hours of deal closing
- Payment processed within 14 days
- Partner receives payment confirmation via email

### 5. PARTNER OBLIGATIONS

**5.1 Ethical Conduct**
- Partner will represent Company honestly and professionally
- Partner will not make false promises or misrepresent properties
- Partner will respect client privacy and confidentiality

**5.2 Marketing Guidelines**
- Partner may use approved marketing materials only
- Partner will not create independent advertising without approval
- Partner will not spam or use unsolicited marketing tactics

**5.3 Information Accuracy**
- Partner will provide accurate referral information
- Partner will update contact details if changes occur
- Partner will notify Company of any conflicts of interest

### 6. COMPANY OBLIGATIONS

**6.1 Commission Payment**
- Company will pay commissions according to terms
- Company will provide referral status updates
- Company will maintain accurate commission records

**6.2 Support**
- Company will provide marketing materials
- Company will offer training and resources
- Company will maintain partner support channel

**6.3 Transparency**
- Company will provide referral tracking access
- Company will communicate program changes
- Company will resolve disputes fairly

### 7. PROGRAM RULES

**7.1 Referral Period**
- Referrals valid for 90 days from registration
- Extensions possible with written agreement

**7.2 Territory**
- Program currently limited to Abuja, Nigeria
- Expansion to other cities announced separately

**7.3 Exclusivity**
- Partner may work with other referral programs
- Partner may not refer same client to multiple agents

### 8. TERMINATION

**8.1 By Partner**
- Partner may terminate with 30 days written notice
- Outstanding commissions paid for valid referrals

**8.2 By Company**
- Company may terminate for breach of agreement
- Company may terminate with 30 days notice
- Fraud or misconduct results in immediate termination

**8.3 Post-Termination**
- Referrals made before termination remain valid
- Commissions earned before termination paid as scheduled
- Partner must cease using Company materials

### 9. CONFIDENTIALITY

**9.1 Information Protection**
- Partner agrees to protect client information
- Partner will not share proprietary Company information
- Partner will not disclose commission rates to third parties

**9.2 Data Usage**
- Company will use Partner data per privacy policy
- Company will not sell Partner information
- Company will maintain data security

### 10. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

**10.1 Company Liability**
- Company not liable for Partner's independent actions
- Company not responsible for referral decisions
- Maximum liability limited to commissions owed

**10.2 Partner Liability**
- Partner liable for own marketing activities
- Partner responsible for tax obligations
- Partner indemnifies Company for misrepresentation

### 11. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

**11.1 Negotiation**
- Parties will attempt good-faith negotiation
- Written notice of dispute required

**11.2 Mediation**
- If negotiation fails, mediation in Abuja
- Costs shared equally

**11.3 Arbitration**
- Final resolution through Nigerian arbitration
- Binding on both parties

### 12. GENERAL PROVISIONS

**12.1 Amendment**
- Agreement may be amended with written consent
- Company may update program terms with 30 days notice

**12.2 Assignment**
- Partner may not assign agreement without consent
- Company may assign to successor entity

**12.3 Governing Law**
- Agreement governed by Nigerian law
- Jurisdiction: Federal Capital Territory, Abuja

**12.4 Entire Agreement**
- This document constitutes entire agreement
- Supersedes prior oral or written agreements

**12.5 Severability**
- Invalid provisions severed without affecting remainder
- Parties will negotiate replacement provisions
