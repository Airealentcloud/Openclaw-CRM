## Usage Notes

**Personalization Fields:**
- [Name] — Partner or referral name
- [Partner Name] — Partner's full name
- [XXXXX] — Partner/referral ID numbers
- [Amount] — Monetary values
- [Date] — Specific dates

**Sending Schedule:**
- Invitation: As needed
- Welcome: Immediately after registration
- Referral confirmation: Within 1 hour of referral
- Commission notification: Within 48 hours of payment
- Newsletter: Monthly, 1st of month
- Re-engagement: After 60 days of inactivity

**Tone Guidelines:**
- Professional but friendly
- Clear and concise
- Action-oriented
- Appreciative of partner efforts
