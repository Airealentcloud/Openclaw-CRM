## 🎯 Program Overview

### Mission
Turn satisfied clients and industry contacts into active sales partners by rewarding successful property referrals.

### Why It Works
- **Trust transfer**: Referrals come pre-qualified through trusted sources
- **Lower CAC**: Customer acquisition cost drops by 50-70% vs. paid ads
- **Network effect**: One happy client can introduce 3-5 new prospects
- **Scalable**: No limit to how many partners you can have

## 💰 Commission Structure

### Tier 1: Standard Partner (Individual Referrers)
**Who qualifies:** Past clients, friends, family, general public

| Property Type | Commission | Example |
|--------------|------------|---------|
| Land (₦10M - ₦50M) | 2% | ₦200K - ₦1M |
| Apartment (₦30M - ₦80M) | 2.5% | ₦750K - ₦2M |
| Duplex (₦80M - ₦200M) | 3% | ₦2.4M - ₦6M |
| Luxury (₦200M+) | 3.5% | ₦7M+ |

**Payment timeline:** 14 days after deal closes and client pays full amount

### Tier 2: Professional Partner (Agents/Brokers)
**Who qualifies:** Licensed agents, other brokers, property consultants

| Property Type | Commission | Example |
|--------------|------------|---------|
| All properties | 4% | Based on sale price |
| Bulk deals (3+ units) | 5% | Negotiable |

**Additional benefits:**
- Priority access to new listings
- Co-branding opportunities
- Dedicated partner support line
- Quarterly partner meetings

### Tier 3: Strategic Partner (Developers/Institutions)
**Who qualifies:** Estate developers, banks, investment firms, diaspora associations

| Deal Type | Commission/Structure |
|-----------|---------------------|
| Developer partnerships | 5% or flat fee per unit |
| Bank referrals | 3% + co-marketing |
| Diaspora groups | 4% + group discount |
| Corporate relocation | Custom negotiated |

## 📋 Program Terms & Conditions

### Eligibility Requirements

**Individual Referrers:**
- Must be 18+ years old
- Valid ID and bank account
- No real estate license required
- Cannot refer themselves

**Professional Partners:**
- Valid real estate license (where applicable)
- Business registration
- Professional indemnity insurance preferred
- Clean industry record

### Referral Rules

**Valid Referrals:**
- New client not previously in A.I Realent database
- Referral registered before first contact
- Deal closes within 90 days of referral
- Full payment received

**Invalid Referrals:**
- Existing clients or past inquiries
- Self-referrals
- Referrals without prior registration
- Deals that don't close

### Commission Payment Terms

**Payment Schedule:**
1. Referral registers → Acknowledgment within 24 hours
2. Deal closes → Notification within 48 hours
3. Full payment received → Commission paid within 14 days
4. Payment method: Bank transfer only

**Deductions:**
- 5% withholding tax (as per Nigerian tax law)
- No other deductions

## 📱 Referral Process

### Step 1: Partner Registration
**How it works:**
1. Prospect visits: airealent.ng/partners
2. Fills registration form:
   - Full name
   - Email
   - Phone
   - Bank details
   - Referral tier preference
3. Receives unique partner ID: AIP-XXXXX
4. Gets welcome email with toolkit

### Step 2: Making Referrals
**Partner actions:**
1. Shares unique referral link or gives partner ID
2. Prospect mentions partner ID on first contact
3. Or prospect uses referral link to browse properties

**Tracking methods:**
- Unique referral links (auto-tracking)
- Partner ID code (manual entry)
- WhatsApp referral registration
- Phone call mention

### Step 3: Deal Progress
**Partner visibility:**
- Partner receives email updates:
  - Referral contacted ✓
  - Property viewed ✓
  - Offer made ✓
  - Deal closed ✓
  - Commission paid ✓

### Step 4: Commission Payment
**Process:**
1. Deal closes
2. Client completes payment
3. Finance verifies commission amount
4. Payment processed within 14 days
5. Partner receives confirmation email

## ⚠️ Risk Management

### Potential Issues & Solutions

**Solution:** Clear terms, written agreements, dispute resolution process

**Issue:** Partner inactivity
**Solution:** Regular engagement, reactivation campaigns, tier demotion

**Solution:** Quality scoring, partner education, tier adjustments

**Issue:** Tax compliance
**Solution:** Automatic withholding, annual tax documents, legal consultation
