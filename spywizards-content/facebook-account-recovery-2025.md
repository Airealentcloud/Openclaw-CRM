# Facebook Account Recovery 2025: Complete Guide to Regaining Access

Losing access to your Facebook account can be a stressful experience. Whether you've forgotten your password, been locked out, or suspect unauthorized access, this comprehensive guide will walk you through the official recovery process and help you secure your account for the future.

## Why Facebook Account Recovery Matters

Your Facebook account often contains years of memories, important connections, and potentially sensitive communications. Understanding the recovery process before you need it can save you significant stress and potential data loss.

## Official Facebook Recovery Methods

### Method 1: Password Reset via Email or Phone

The most straightforward recovery method involves using your registered email or phone number:

1. Go to facebook.com/login
2. Click "Forgotten password?"
3. Enter your email or phone number
4. Follow the verification steps
5. Create a new strong password

### Method 2: Trusted Contacts Recovery

If you've set up trusted contacts, you can use this feature:

1. Select "Forgot account?" on the login page
2. Search for your account using your email or phone
3. Choose "No longer have access to these?"
4. Enter a new email or phone number
5. Contact your trusted contacts for recovery codes

### Method 3: Identity Verification

For more complex cases, Facebook may require identity verification:

- Submit government-issued ID
- Provide a selfie for facial recognition
- Answer security questions
- Verify associated email or phone numbers

## Preventing Future Lockouts

- Enable two-factor authentication
- Keep recovery information updated
- Save login codes in a secure password manager
- Set up login alerts
